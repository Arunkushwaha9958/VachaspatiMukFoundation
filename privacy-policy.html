Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025

← Go Back

Vachaspati MUK Foundation ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or make a donation.

Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you make a donation, contact us, subscribe to updates, or fill out any form. This may include your full name, email address, phone number, mailing address, and PAN card number (for 80G tax receipts).

How We Use Your Information

We use the information to process donations and issue receipts, send 80G tax exemption certificates, communicate program updates, respond to your queries, and comply with legal obligations.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data with trusted payment processors solely to complete your donation. All such partners are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of internet transmission is 100% secure.

Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect some site functionality.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data at any time. Contact us at info@vachaspatimukfoundation.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
